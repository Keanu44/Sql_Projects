# Restaurant Order & Menu Analysis (SQL Project)

## Project Overview

- This SQL project analyzes a restaurant’s menu data and order transaction data to uncover insights about:

* Pricing distribution

- Category performance

- Order behavior

- High-value customers

- Most and least popular items

- The goal is to simulate a real-world business scenario where stakeholders ask:

**Which categories perform best?**

**What do our highest-spending customers order?**

**How large are our typical orders?**

## Objectives & Business Questions

### Objective 1: Menu Exploration

- Total number of menu items

- Minimum and maximum prices

- Most & least expensive items

- Category-level dish count

- Average price per category

- Italian dish pricing breakdown

📊 Business Insight:
This helps management understand pricing strategy and category positioning.

### Objective 2: Order Analysis

- Order date range

- Total number of orders

- Total number of items ordered

- Orders with the most items

- Orders with more than 12 items

📊 Business Insight:
This helps identify bulk purchasing behavior and demand intensity.

## 📈 Business Value

- This project demonstrates:

- Relational data analysis

- Revenue-based ranking logic

- Customer behavior analysis

- Category-level pricing insights

- Practical business storytelling using SQL
